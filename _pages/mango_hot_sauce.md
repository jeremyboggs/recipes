# Mango Hot Sauce

Courtesy of [Jim Safley](http://jimsafley.com).

## Ingredients

-   2-3 mangos, diced
-   1 habanero pepper
-   Salt, to taste

## Directions

Puree the mangos and habanero until smooth. Cook on stove,
medium/medium-low heat, for 20-30 minutes, adding salt to taste. Pour in
jar and refrigerate.
