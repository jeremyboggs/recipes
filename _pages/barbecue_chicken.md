# Barbecue Chicken

-   1 lb chicken tenders (cut up and boil)
-   1/4 cup minced sweet onion
-   2tbs butter to sautee onions
-   3tbs vinegar
-   1cup ketchup
-   1 cup water
-   2tsp brown sugar
-   1tsp dry mustard
-   1tsp salt
-   1/2tsp black pepper.

Combine ingredients, bake at 300 for one hour.
