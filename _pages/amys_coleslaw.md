# Amy's Coleslaw

## Directions

Combine the following ingredients and refrigerate:

-   8 Cups cabbage, finely chopped
-   1/4 Cup shredded carrot
-   1/3 Cup sugar
-   1/2 tsp. salt
-   1/8 tsp. pepper
-   1/4 Cup milk
-   1/4 Cup buttermilk
-   1/2 Cup mayonnaise
-   1 1/2 tsp. vinegar
-   2 2/1 Tbs. lemon juice
