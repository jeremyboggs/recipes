# Amy's Dressing

## Ingredients

-   1 Cup sugar
-   1 cup boiling water
-   1 16oz. bottle Creamy Italian dressing
-   2-4 Tbs. red wine vinegar
-   2-4 Tbs. salad supreme.

## Directions

Dissolve the sugar in the boiling water, and let the water cool. Combine
remaining ingredients and refrigerate.
